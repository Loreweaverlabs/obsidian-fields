The Obsidian Fields

You captain a free mercenary company on a black-glass battlefield where two undead powers — the Lich Lord and the Zombie King — are grinding each other to dust. You never see a battle: you read your lieutenants' reports each morning, decide what (and whom) to believe, and give the next day's orders.

How to play

  • 30 turns, about an hour. Your progress saves in the browser after every turn — you can leave and come back.
  • Each turn: read the Reports, then issue orders at the Council, then end the turn.
  • Take contracts to pay the wages. Manage your five lieutenants however you see fit.
  • Trust whom you will.

One request

The cast is the same for everyone — please don't compare notes with other testers until after your debrief. Your first read of these people is the whole experiment.

When you finish

  1. On the final screen, press Export this run.
  2. Copy the text (or download the file) and send it to the project lead along with anything you want to say.
  3. The lead will follow up with a few questions about how it went — soon after, while it's fresh.

The export is a replay of your decisions — no personal data, just the campaign.

Works in any modern browser, phone or desktop. Nothing to install.
